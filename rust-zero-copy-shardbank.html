MVT 3c: Zero-Copy Rust over a Shared Wasm-Memory Shard Bank

This prototype upgrades the shard bank from plain SharedArrayBuffer shards to shared WebAssembly.Memory shards. DuckDB-Wasm query results are routed into that bank as Arrow IPC bytes, then 4 Rust/Wasm workers read those bytes directly in place from the imported shard memories without a copy.
