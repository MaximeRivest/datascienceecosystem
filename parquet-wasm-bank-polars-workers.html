Parquet-Wasm -> Bank -> Polars Workers

This is the closest currently implementable non-DuckDB Rust/Wasm ingest alternative. Instead of DuckDB-Wasm scanning the Parquet file, parquet-wasm reads the local file in Rust/Wasm, emits Arrow RecordBatches, routes selected columns into the shared bank, and then runs fresh live workers and fresh Polars workers over the routed bank.

Important nuance: this is not Polars native Parquet IO yet. It is the strongest practical "no DuckDB" ingest/routing follow-on with a Rust/Wasm parquet reader in the browser.
